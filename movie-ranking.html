Aliah's Favourite Movies of All Time

Not just my favourite movies but the actual best movies of all time. If you don't think so...you're wrong.

1. Harry Potter Series

Honestly, need I say more? Magical, spell-binding and just brings you into another world.

2. Inception

Messes with your mind in a good way! Was rethinking the ending and looking for theories for days after.

3. Grease

I don't quite know why this movie has a chokehold on me. But I truly love and look forward to this movie when it's on! Tunes and grooves all round.
